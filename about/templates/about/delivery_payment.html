Delivery and payment:

When placing an order and entering your details, you will receive a CONFIRMATION by phone or email about the order details, delivery times to your address
and the final cost of services to your city within 24 hours, standard order processing takes 5 hours.

Orders are accepted ROUND THE CLOCK and are processed from 8.00 to 22.00 (on weekends and holidays, order processing time may increase!)

DELIVERY TIMES and METHODS:
• By courier to your door: courier delivery in the Republic of Belarus on the day of order, or the next day.
• Euromail: delivery within the Republic of Belarus to points located in Euroopt grocery stores - takes 1 - 3 days.
• Belposhta: delivery within the Republic of Belarus to the Belposhta branch, delivery to Russia and the EU is also available - takes 2-10 working days.

PAYMENT METHODS:
• Cash upon receipt of goods;
• Visa, Mastercard, MIR, BelCard and others;
• Installment cards "Halva", "Shopping Card", "Turtle" and other installment cards;
• Assist Belarus (online payment on the website through a certified service);
• Apple pay and Samsung pay (select payment method: Card online on the website Assist Belarus);
• Payment by QR code (you will be provided with a link to pay through your bank).
